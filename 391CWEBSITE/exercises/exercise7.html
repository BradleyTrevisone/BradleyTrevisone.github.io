Writing Samples

All samples are from my Undergraduates at: Umass Amherst

Shakespeare Essay on Antony and Cleopatra
This essay explores the meaning behind the word 'lament' and discovers who is actually doing the action and why it's significant.

Shakespeare Essay on Hamlet
This assignment explores how people understand the unknown by applying conceptual ideas beyond their knowledge or universal feelings that are misapplied.

Shakespeare Essay on King Lear
This essay explores how Shakespeare uses King Lear to create a commentary on human nature; specifically about how we unknowingly surrendered a sacred part of our identity in order to survive in this world.

Comparing enacted law in Shakespeare
An essay exploring the differences of how law is enacted in tragedies vs comedies using Titus Andronicus and A Midsummer Night dream.
