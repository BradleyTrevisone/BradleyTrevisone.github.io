Value In Removing Stimulus

Removing and limiting stimulating inputs will lead you to a happier, calmer, and more appretiative life.
It begins with understanding dopamine as a motivational chemical: not a happiness chemical.

An Easy Experiment

Next time you find youself struggling to begin a task, start by putting any imediate destractions away.
This includes such as your phone, music, and even acsessive caffine bevrages. Reset your motivation
levels by taking a 5 minute break to sit in silence while either looking out of a window or even a wall
that is at least 15 away from you.
When you reengage in your work, watch as your body begins to engage deeper as your body attempts
to restore its dopamine levels with this new task.

Please note that while this is helpful for your productivity, it's applicable to your daily life aswell.

Benifits

  1. It makes switching between tasks an easier transition
  2. You will be more focused while working on your task
  3. You may find yourself wanting to engage more within your enviroment,
    such as creating conversations with people close with you or finding more time in your day to
    work on projects.
  4. Your body begins to value simplicities as its not overwhelmed by
    massive dopamine spikes that comes from a continous search for entertainment
